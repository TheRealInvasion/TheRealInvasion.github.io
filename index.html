How to Connect to a Server
  1. Launch Squad (either from Steam or by clicking the [Launch Game] button below).
  2. Once the game is running, minimize it by pressing ALT+TAB (don't close it).
  3. Come back here and click Connect on any server in the list.

Important: The game must already be open before you press Connect.
